---
title: WORKSPACE_NOT_FOUND
description: The associated workspace could not be found or is deactivated.
---

## What does this mean?
The workspace corresponding to the API key you entered does not exist in our system, or it has been deactivated due to account suspension or deletion.

## How to verify?
Ensure that the API key you are using was issued from the correct account.

## How to fix?
1. Verify if you can log in to the Pavy Admin normally.
2. Check your account's subscription and payment status. Services may be suspended due to non-payment.
3. If you recently created a new workspace, ensure you aren't still using the key from a previous workspace.

![Admin → Workspace status screen capture](/images/troubleshoot/workspace-not-found-admin.svg)

## Still stuck?
If your account status is normal but you still receive this error, please contact our [Support Team](mailto:support@pavy.ai).
