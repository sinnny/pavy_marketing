## What does this mean?
CORS (Cross-Origin Resource Sharing) is a security policy that limits requests to other domains. This happens when the Pavy server does not recognize your current domain as a trusted one.

## How to verify?
Check the **Console** or **Network** tab in Developer Tools for error messages related to `Access-Control-Allow-Origin`.

## How to fix?
1. **Register Domain:** In most cases, this is caused by not registering your domain in [Admin > Domain Management]. Refer to the [ORIGIN_NOT_ALLOWED Guide](./origin-not-allowed) to register your domain.
2. **Check Protocol:** `http` and `https` are considered different domains. In production, ensure you are using `https` and have registered the domain with the `https://` prefix.

![CORS error example in DevTools Console](/images/troubleshoot/cors-blocked-devtools.svg)

## Still stuck?
If testing in a local development environment (`localhost`), ensure you have explicitly added `http://localhost:3000` or similar to the Admin panel.
