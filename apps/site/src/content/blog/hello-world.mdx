# Introducing Pavy.ai

Welcome to the future of e-commerce interaction. **Pavy.ai** is not just another chatbot; it's a sales-driven AI agent designed to understand your customers and guide them to the perfect product.

## Why Pavy.ai?

E-commerce has evolved, but the way we help customers find products hasn't. Static filters and basic search are no longer enough. Customers want a conversational experience that feels like talking to a knowledgeable store assistant.

### Key Features

1.  **AI Product Recommendations**: Understands intent, not just keywords.
2.  **Multilingual Support**: Sell globally with native-level conversations in Korean, Japanese, and English.
3.  **Seamless Integration**: Works with Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom platforms.
